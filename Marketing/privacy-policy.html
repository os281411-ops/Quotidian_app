Privacy Policy

Quotidian for iOS — Last updated July 21, 2026

Quotidian is a simple daily-quote app. This policy explains, plainly, what happens to your data — which is very little.

Short version

Quotidian does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to us or to any third party. There is no account, no analytics, no tracking, and no advertising in the app.

What stays on your device

The app saves a small amount of information locally, using Apple's standard on-device storage. None of it ever leaves your phone or is visible to us:

  • Your current and longest daily streak
  • Quotes you've chosen to save to your Library
  • Whether you've turned on the daily reminder, and what time you set

If you delete the app, this data is deleted with it.

Notifications

The optional daily reminder is a local notification scheduled entirely on your device. Nothing about your usage of the app is sent anywhere to make this work.

Links to Bookshop.org

Tapping "Purchase Book" opens a book's page on Bookshop.org in your browser. Once you leave Quotidian, your activity is covered by Bookshop.org's own privacy policy, not this one. We are not able to see or access anything you do there.

Children's privacy

Because Quotidian doesn't collect any data from anyone, it doesn't knowingly collect data from children either.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the update will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to quotidian.support@gmail.com.
