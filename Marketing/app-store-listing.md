## Description (4000 char max)
Quotidian brings you one carefully chosen quote a day — pulled from sixty of the world's great novels, memoirs, and biographies, from Moby-Dick to Beloved, Frankenstein to Becoming.

Each quote comes with a short note on the book it's from, so a single line can lead you to your next great read.

FEATURES

• Daily Quote — a new passage every day, the same for everyone, so there's always something to talk about
• Library — save the quotes that stop you in your tracks and revisit them anytime
• Streaks — keep a daily reading habit alive, with milestone celebrations at 7, 30, 100, and 365 days
• Reminders — an optional daily nudge so you never miss today's quote
• Shop the Book — every quote links straight to that book on Bookshop.org, supporting independent bookstores with every purchase

Sixty books and counting, spanning classic literature, philosophy, and modern memoir — Moby-Dick, Pride and Prejudice, 1984, Beloved, The Kite Runner, Man's Search for Meaning, and more.

Quotidian doesn't collect any personal data, doesn't require an account, and doesn't track you. It's just a quote, every day.

## Age Rating
No explicit sexual content, drug use, or graphic violence is depicted — quotes are short literary excerpts. Some source books deal with serious historical subjects (the Holocaust, slavery, war), referenced only in brief, non-graphic quotations. In Apple's age rating questionnaire, this most likely maps to "Infrequent/Mild" for Mature/Suggestive Themes, typically resulting in a 12+ rating. Fill this in yourself in App Store Connect — it's a self-certification and the final call is yours.

## App Privacy (Data Collection)
Quotidian collects no data at all: no analytics, no accounts, no third-party SDKs, no network calls except opening Bookshop.org links in the browser (which the user explicitly taps). In App Store Connect's Privacy section, this means you can answer "Data Not Collected" for the whole app.
