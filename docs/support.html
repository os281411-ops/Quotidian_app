How streaks work

Your streak counts consecutive calendar days you've opened the app. Miss a day and it resets to 1. Milestones at 7, 30, 100, and 365 days get a special celebration.

Daily reminders

Turn on the daily reminder from the Profile tab to get a nudge at a time you choose. You can change or turn off the reminder time anytime in the same place.

Saving quotes

Tap the bookmark icon on any quote to add it to your Library. Saved quotes stay on your device and aren't tied to an account.

Buying a book

Every quote links to that book on Bookshop.org, which supports independent bookstores with every purchase.
